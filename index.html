AccessAble is dedicated to showcasing accessibility tools and resources for a more inclusive web. Our mission is to help everyone, including people with disabilities, access and enjoy the digital world.

Explore Accessibility Resources

What is Web Accessibility?

Web accessibility means designing and developing websites, tools, and technologies so that people with disabilities can use them. This includes making sure everyone can perceive, understand, navigate, and interact with the web.
